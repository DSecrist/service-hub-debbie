There are several causes for A/C failure in a BMW vehicle and to properly solve the issue, it is important to figure out the source of the problem.

As such, BMW Certified Technicians start by looking for the following issues when performing a diagnosis on your car:
    Weak Air Flow
    The compressor causes the air to blow through the A/C vents. If the air coming through the vents is damp or lacks pressure as it blows out, our BMW Certified Technician will know that it needs to be repaired.

    Room Temperature Air
    If your A/C is blowing out warm air or alternating hot and cold air, this may be an indication that the system is low on freon.

    Reduced Air Circulation
    Broken or malfunctioning fans will reduce how much air is coming out of the vents. If you feel weak airflow or little circulation, a fan issue may be to blame.
