Road trips are a great way to see the country and make memories with your family and friends. But hitting the highway can turn an epic adventure into a headache. A pre-trip checkup can ensure that your BMW is road-ready, so you and your loved ones are safe.

That’s why it’s a good idea to let a BMW Certified Technician prepare your car for the trip. The full service includes:
    Brakes
    Checking your brake system, including the brake pad and rotor, to ensure that it is in optimum working condition.

    Tires
    Inspecting the tires for pressure and tread. Underinflated tires can reduce your fuel economy while worn-out tires need a wheel alignment more often than necessary. BMW Certified Technicians will check for bald spots and bulges on all four tires and suggest the right solution.

    Engine Oil
    Check for oil levels and see if the car is due for an oil change soon.

    Inspect Hoses and Belts
    These can become brittle, loose or frayed with time, affecting the electrical, air conditioning, power steering and cooling systems in your car. You wouldn’t want to be caught on the highway if your car breaks down. A BMW Certified Technician will look at these before you take your car out for the long drive.

    Air Conditioning
    Double-check the coolant levels and refill them as necessary. Also replace air filters to ensure that your A/C continues blowing air free of debris.

    Wipers Check
    Replace the wiper blades and fluid if necessary, so you can have maximum visibility even when driving.
Bring your car in for a comprehensive road-readiness test to ensure that you and your loved ones have the best road trip.
