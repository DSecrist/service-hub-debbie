Car batteries need replacement every few years. Continuous cycles of charging-discharging-charging cause depletion in its plates over time. However, several key indicators will help you identify if your battery may need to be replaced soon. If you experience any difficulty with your BMW’s electrical system, it may be time for a battery replacement.
    Longer Engine Start Times
    A drained battery can may it difficult for the engine to turn-over. As the key is turned in the ignition, your engine should start immediately. If you notice any gap of time between push-starting or turning the key, your battery efficiency may be reaching the end of its life.

    Dimming Headlights
    Dimming headlights is a good indicator for battery has reduced charge. Try turning on your vehicle in the early hours of the morning or night when it is dark. If you notice your headlights dim for a short time, then increase in brightness, it may be time for a battery replacement.

    Only Dashboard Lights Work
    If you try turning on your vehicle and only see the dashboard lights and notifications illuminated, your vehicle’s battery only has enough energy to power the display and nothing beyond that.
