It is recommended that your wiper blades are changed seasonally, as the wiper blades receive more strain during the colder months. If your wiper blades cannot clear your windows, and you cannot remember the last time you had them replaced, have a BMW Certified Technician inspect them as soon as possible. Our skilled technicians use these criteria to know when to replace your windshield wiper blades and maximize their effectiveness:
  • Ineffectiveness in rainy or snowy weather
  • Streaks or splotches on your windshield
  • Squeaking or scrapping sounds while operating
  • Splits, jagged edges or discoloration in the rubber
  • Occasional sticking to your vehicleâ€™s windshield
To develop a system of replacing your wiper blades at routine intervals, choose to have them replaced during routine maintenance services, such as emissions testing or during oil changes. Every time you have your blades replaced by our BMW Certified Technicians, also be sure to ask them to top off your wiper fluid levels.
